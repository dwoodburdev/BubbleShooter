Description

Emoji Pop is an incredibly addicting puzzle game that also transforms its players into level designers. Its gameplay is similar to popular Match-3 games - shoot your emoji-bubble to make a match. However, unlike traditional puzzle games, Emoji Pop also comes with a Level Creator, a series of level design tutorials, and a community obsessed with making fun levels.

History

While starting off as a level designer in the mobile games industry, Dylan Woodbury learned that creating levels was a deeper, more enriching activity than just playing levels in a standard mobile game. In 2018, Woodbury left the industry to pursue independent development. Emoji Pop is his attempt to give players that amazing experience he had designing levels for mobile games. The game releases for Web on Emoji Day (July 17), and will be contantly updated in the months to follow, leading up to an iOS/Android release later this year.

Features

  • A professional Level Creator that gives players the tools to create whatever they want.
  • Video tutorials train players to use the Level Creator and to learn the fundamentals of game deisgn.
  • Beat levels to rank up and gain new abilities ini your Level Creator.
  • Publish a level and watch your friends compete for the high score.
  • Challenge someone to beat your level by sending them a link. They don't even need an account.
  • Climb the list of the top creators by getting likes and followers.
  • Make a really cool level? It might get featured in the game.
  • This is your chance to become a published level designer. The best levels will be featured in the upcoming mobile game.
